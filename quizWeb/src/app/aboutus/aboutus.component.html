About Our Online Exam System

Features of Our Online Exam System

  • Secure and user-friendly interface
  • Timed exams with auto-submit functionality
  • Instant result generation
  • Multiple-choice, descriptive, and coding questions
  • Role-based access for students, teachers, and admins
  • Automated grading system for objective questions
  • Live monitoring and anti-cheating mechanisms

Exam Guidelines

  • Log in 10 minutes before the exam starts
  • No switching between tabs or browsers
  • Ensure a stable internet connection
  • Use a webcam-enabled device for live monitoring
  • Follow instructions provided by the invigilator
  • Exams will auto-submit once time runs out

Requirements for Taking the Exam

  • A laptop or desktop with a functioning webcam
  • Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox browser
  • Minimum internet speed of 2 Mbps
  • Quiet and well-lit environment
  • Valid login credentials for access

Exam Details

The exam format includes:

  • Multiple-choice questions (MCQs)
  • Short answer and essay-type questions
  • Programming-based questions for coding exams
  • Time-based and open-book options
  • Results and performance analytics available
